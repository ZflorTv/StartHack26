# Game Vision

> *Growden transforms the intimidating world of investing into a living garden — teaching players to build, protect, and grow a portfolio they can see, touch, and feel.*

---

## 🌟 The Fantasy

Imagine you're not staring at spreadsheets and stock tickers. Instead, you're tending a garden.

Your apple trees are financial stocks. Your cacti are commodities. Your orchids are crypto. When a storm rolls in — that's a market crash. When the sun shines — that's an earnings beat. Your job isn't to predict the weather. It's to build a garden that thrives in any season.

**Growden sells the fantasy of becoming a wise, calm investor** — someone who doesn't panic when lightning strikes, who knows that the right mix of plants will weather any storm. It transforms the abstract terror of "investing" into something tangible, visual, and even beautiful.

The player feels like a **gardener-strategist** — part botanist, part financial advisor, watching their choices bloom or wilt in real time.

## 🔄 The Loop

**The One-Sentence Pitch:** Build a garden of financial plants, survive market weather events, learn to invest by watching your choices grow or die.

Players choose a gardening style (conservative, balanced, or aggressive), plant their first assets, and immediately face their first market event. They watch the weather change. They see their plants react. They decide: hold steady, rebalance, or panic sell. Then they see the score — and they learn.

This loop repeats across 10 levels, each introducing new plant types, new weather events, and higher stakes. A full session takes 10–15 minutes. But the lessons — diversification, patience, risk management — are designed to last a lifetime.

## 🎲 The Tension & Drama

The game is built for **PostFinance** and the Swiss financial education market. It targets a specific, underserved audience: **young adults (18–35) who know they should invest but find traditional financial education boring, intimidating, or irrelevant.**

Growden meets them where they are — on their phones, in 15-minute sessions, with a visual language they already understand from mobile gaming. It doesn't lecture. It lets them fail safely, then explains why.

## 🧠 The Psychology

What makes Growden unlike anything else in the financial education space is its **metaphor engine**. Other apps teach investing through quizzes, articles, or paper trading. Growden teaches through *lived experience inside a garden.*

- A stock crash isn't a red number. It's a **tornado ripping through your cherry blossoms**.
- Diversification isn't a textbook concept. It's **watching your cacti thrive during a drought that killed your orchids**.
- Panic selling isn't a mistake you read about. It's a **button you pressed — and the -50 points burned into your score**.

This isn't gamification bolted onto education. It's education dissolved into gameplay. The metaphor is the method.

## 💰 The Business Connection

**For PostFinance:** Growden is a customer acquisition and financial literacy tool. Every player who learns to invest through Growden is a potential PostFinance customer who now understands — and trusts — the concepts behind the bank's products.

**For the Market:** The global financial literacy app market is growing rapidly, but most solutions are either too dry (courses) or too risky (paper trading with real data). Growden occupies a unique position: **emotionally engaging, zero real-world risk, and directly transferable learning**.

**Revenue Paths:** Event activations (conferences, schools), B2B licensing to banks and educational institutions, freemium consumer app with expanded content, and branded partnership opportunities.

## ⚠️ Design Gaps & Open Questions

- ⚠️ **Design Gap** — *How does the game handle players who already know investing? Is there a "skip tutorial" path that still feels rewarding?*
- ⚠️ **Design Gap** — *The AI coach Flora is designed but runs on hardcoded hints. Full Claude API integration would dramatically deepen personalization — strategic decision needed on investment.*
- ⚠️ **Design Gap** — *Multiplayer is designed for Firebase but not yet implemented. The social layer is currently thin — is the solo experience enough for retention?*

## 🔮 Design Opportunities

1. **Flora AI Coach (Claude-Powered):** Real-time, personalized financial coaching that adapts to the player's portfolio decisions. "You just panic-sold during a moderate dip — here's why that cost you, and what you could do next time." This transforms a static game into a dynamic mentor.

2. **Real Financial Data Integration:** Connect plant performance to actual market indices. Players could see how their garden would have performed during the 2008 crisis, the COVID crash, or the 2022 rate hike cycle. History becomes playable.

3. **Institutional Deployment Kit:** Package Growden as a turnkey financial literacy tool for schools, banks, and corporate training programs — with dashboards showing aggregate learning outcomes and engagement metrics.
